## mberry (Mulberry 桑葚)

5-star Medic (Single-Target). 2 skills. Source: arknights.wiki.gg + prts.wiki confirmed.

- S1 "Healing Haze": sp=0/0, dur=3s, AUTO_TIME, AUTO
  - Charge-based (up to 2 stored). Fires automatically after a heal charges it.
  - sp_cost=0 means it charges passively from time; triggered without player input.
- S2 "Safe Zone": sp=66/50, dur=30s, AUTO_TIME, MANUAL
  - Significantly reduces attack interval; all allies in range take 35% less Elemental Injury.

Codebase stub had S1 sp=9/init=0 and S2 sp=40/init=20 — both wrong. Corrected values above.

## melnte (Melanite)

5-star Sniper (Deadeye). 2 skills. Source: arknights.wiki.gg confirmed.

- S1 "Saturated Pulse": sp=40/25, dur=30s, AUTO_TIME, MANUAL
  - Increases attack interval; ATK +180% during active window.
- S2 "Critical Burst": sp=15/6, dur=0s (instant), AUTO_TIME, MANUAL
  - Railgun piercing shot: 500% ATK physical damage to all enemies in a line (up to 4 tiles).

S1 is NOT AUTO_ATTACK; it's a timed active buff (MANUAL, AUTO_TIME). S2 is instant, not 20s.

### Critical codebase errors found

#### pinecone.py — WRONG SKILLS (major)
The file `/arknights-sim/data/characters/pinecone.py` implements:
- S2 "Heavy Barrage" (sp=35, init=15, dur=20s, ATK+30%, splash)
- S3 "Grand Barrage" (sp=45, init=20, dur=20s, ATK+100%)

These are NOT Pinecone's skills. Pinecone (char_440_pinecn) has:
- S1 "RMA Spikes" (sp=10, init=0, 9s cooldown, charge-based, MANUAL)
- S2 "Electrical Overcharge" (sp=30, init=0, 20s, ATK buff stacking up to +60%, AUTO)
- No S3.

"Heavy Barrage" and "Grand Barrage" are from a different operator (possibly an Artillery archetype operator — they do not belong to char_440). The codebase also incorrectly assigns `RoleArchetype.SNIPER_ARTILLERY` to Pinecone — Pinecone is a standard Deadeye sniper, not Artillery archetype.

#### ptilopsis.py — WRONG SKILLS (major)
The file `/arknights-sim/data/characters/ptilopsis.py` implements:
- S1 "Dawn's Resonance" (sp=25, init=10, 25s, ATK+50%, AUTO)
- S2 "Night Cure" (sp=30, init=12, 20s, ATK+80% + AoE heal, AUTO)
- S3 "Dream Catcher" (sp=35, init=12, 30s, ATK+100%, MANUAL)

None of these skills exist on Ptilopsis (char_128_plosis). Actual skills:
- S1 "Healing Up γ" (sp=30, init=20, 30s, ATK+90%, AUTO_TIME, **MANUAL**)
- S2 "Enkephalin" (sp=100, init=85, 40s, range expands + atk interval -2.1s, AUTO_TIME, **MANUAL**)
- No S3.

The talent implementation ("Unisonant" / Skill Aura giving +0.3 SP/s at E2) is **correct** in concept — the EN wiki calls it "Skill Aura" but the effect is right.
