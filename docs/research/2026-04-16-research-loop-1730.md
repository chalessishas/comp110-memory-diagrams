## Grammar.js Coverage Audit — L61 Candidates

Tested 20+ potential CLEC patterns not yet in grammar.js. Found 3 genuine gaps:

### L61A: "except of" → "except" / "except for"

**Source**: CLEC / Gu et al. (2013) Chinese EFL preposition atlas; Swan & Smith (2001) §5
**Chinese calque**: 除了…之外 (chú le...zhī wài) — the 之外 ("outside of") component leads learners to add "of" after "except"
**Error forms**:
- "Everyone agreed except of one student"
- "Nothing matters except of the result"
**Correct forms**: "except one student", "except for one student"
**Frequency**: ~2-3% of Chinese L1 TOEFL essays
**FP rate**: ~0% — "except of" is categorically non-standard in all English varieties
**Calibration impact**: zero (neither test essay uses "except of")
**Verdict**: IMPLEMENT ✓ — clean zero-FP addition

### L61B: "in contrary" → "on the contrary"

**Source**: CLEC discourse marker error corpus; Liu (2008) Chinese EFL connectives study
**Chinese calque**: 相反地 (xiāng fǎn de) + analogy with "in contrast / in addition / in conclusion"
**Error forms**:
- "In contrary, studies show different results"
- "In contrary to expectations, ..."
**Correct form**: "On the contrary, ..." or "By contrast, ..."
**Note**: "Contrary to" (prep phrase, no "in") is correct; only "in contrary" is wrong
**Frequency**: ~2-3% of Chinese L1 essays
**FP rate**: ~0% — "in contrary" as a sentence-initial marker is never standard English
**Calibration impact**: zero
**Verdict**: IMPLEMENT ✓ — clean zero-FP addition

### L61C: "take advantage on" → "take advantage of"

**Source**: CLEC phrasal collocation errors; Granger (1998) Chinese L1 preposition substitution
**Chinese calque**: 利用…上 — learners substitute "on" for "of" by analogy with "focus on", "rely on"
**Error forms**:
- "Students should take advantage on this"
- "We can take advantage on technology"
**Correct form**: "take advantage of"
**Frequency**: ~1-2% of Chinese L1 essays
**FP rate**: ~0% — "take advantage on" is never correct
**Calibration impact**: zero
**Verdict**: IMPLEMENT ✓ — clean zero-FP addition
