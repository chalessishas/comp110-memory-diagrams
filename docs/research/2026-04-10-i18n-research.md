## OnboardingWizard Root Cause

The `isZh = prefs.language === "zh"` pattern was dead code: the language picker step was removed from the wizard, so `prefs.language` was always `"en"`. ZH-locale users saw English role/goal labels.

**Fix**: Moved role/goal labels to `onboarding.role.*` and `onboarding.goal.*` i18n keys. Component now uses `t(\`onboarding.role.${role.key}\`)` ‚Äî locale-aware via i18n context.
