- **Error**: "such problem" → "such a problem"
- **Source**: 这样的 calque; omits article after "such"
- **Frequency**: ~12–15% Chinese L1 TOEFL — **highest of all candidates**
- **FP rate**: ~2–3% (noun curated list + guards for "such as", "such that", "such a/an")
- **QWK est.**: ~+0.02–0.03

### Priority 2: `familiar to` with agent subject (FAMILIAR_TO_RE)
- **Error**: "I am familiar to this topic" → "I am familiar with this topic"
- **Source**: 熟悉 used symmetrically in Chinese; agent vs stimulus confusion
- **Frequency**: ~5–8% (especially in Academic Discussion tasks)
- **FP rate**: ~1–2% (pronoun-anchored guard: only fires when human pronoun is subject)
- **QWK est.**: ~+0.01–0.015

### Priority 3: `absent in` → `absent from` (ABSENT_IN_RE)
- **Error**: "absent in class" → "absent from class"  
- **Source**: 缺席在 → "absent in" locative transfer
- **Frequency**: ~3–5%
- **FP rate**: ~1% (exclude "absent in mind/spirit/thought")
- **QWK est.**: ~+0.005–0.01

### Priority 4: `for the purpose of + bare infinitive` (FOR_PURPOSE_BARE_RE)
- **Error**: "for the purpose of study" → "for the purpose of studying"
- **Source**: 为了 + bare verb calque; shares root with `by + bare inf`
- **Frequency**: ~4–6%
- **FP rate**: ~0.5%
- **QWK est.**: ~+0.005–0.01
