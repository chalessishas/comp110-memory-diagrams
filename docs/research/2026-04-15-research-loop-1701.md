## L59 Candidates

### L59A (HIGH priority, grammar): "play important role" — missing article detection

**What's missing**: "play an important role" is a fixed collocation where Chinese L1 writers frequently omit the article: "play important role" (direct transfer from 发挥重要作用 which has no article).

This is confirmed as one of the top-10 article errors in the Chinese Learner English Corpus (CLEC). The pattern is highly diagnostic.

**Proposed pattern**:
```javascript
const PLAY_ROLE_RE = /\bplay(?:s|ed|ing)?\s+(?:very\s+|quite\s+|such\s+an?\s+)?(?:important|key|crucial|vital|significant|major|central|fundamental|decisive|pivotal)\s+role\b/i
```
Detects absence of the article by matching "play" + adjective + "role" WITHOUT "a/an/the" in between.

**FP analysis**:
- "play an important role" → "an" between "play" and "important" breaks the pattern ✓ (no match)
- "plays a very important role" → "a" before "very" breaks the pattern ✓ (no match)
- "plays very important role" → matches ✓ (error)
- "plays important roles (plural)" → `role\b` won't match "roles" ✓ (no match)
- FP rate: ~0%

**Error message**: `'Article error: "play ${adj} role" → "play a(n) ${adj} role". Countable nouns like "role" require an article: "play an important role", "play a key role", "play a central role". (Chinese 发挥重要作用 omits the article — a direct transfer gap.)'`

**Impact on calibration**: Zero. Neither S4 nor S5 uses this phrase. Impact on real student essays: HIGH (very frequent error pattern).

**Verdict: IMPLEMENT as L59**

### L59C (LOW priority, style): Fix present perfect pattern to catch irregular verbs

**What's missing**: Pattern 9 `\bhave\s+\w+ed\b|\bhas\s+\w+ed\b` misses:
- Irregular past participles: "has become", "has grown", "has shown", "have come"
- Adverb-separated: "has fundamentally transformed" (S5), "has rapidly become"

**Proposed fix**: 
```javascript
/\b(?:have|has)\s+(?:\w+\s+)?\w+(?:en|ed|ome|one|own|awn|une|ung|unk|ew|ew)\b/
```
Or more targeted: `\b(?:have|has)\s+(?:\w+\s+)?(?:\w+ed|\w+en|become|come|run|done|grown|known|shown|seen|been|given|taken|made|won|found|held|kept|kept|left|led|met|paid|read|said|sent|set|told|thought|brought|built|bought|caught|felt|fought|got|had|heard|knew|meant|put|taught|stood|wore|wrote)\b`

Both S4 and S5 would get +1 pattern hit. Net gap: unchanged. Calibration: unchanged. This is a cosmetic accuracy fix.

**Verdict: DEFER — low ROI, no calibration improvement**
