### Loop 38 Grammar — Chinese L1 Transfer Errors Not Yet Covered

Based on corpus research gaps from prior loops:

**P1: `make + ADJ` instead of `make + N + ADJ`**
- Pattern: "make difficult" → should be "make it difficult"
- Chinese: 使困难 → English drops extraposition pronoun
- Frequency: ~5-7% in CLEC essays; FP risk: low (check for noun between make and adj)
- Regex: `/\bmake\s+(very\s+)?(difficult|easy|possible|clear|obvious|important|necessary)\b/i`

**P1: Missing article before singular countable nouns (common category)**
- Pattern: "the government should take _ action" (missing "an" or "the")
- Already partially covered? Check grammar.js for article detection
- High frequency but high FP risk — only safe for closed-set common collocations

**P2: `suggest + infinitive` (Chinese 建议 + 动词)**
- Pattern: "I suggest you to reconsider" → should be "I suggest you reconsider" or "I suggest reconsidering"
- Frequency: 4-6%; FP near zero (to-infinitive after suggest is always wrong)
- Regex: `/\bsuggest(?:s|ed)?\s+(?:\w+\s+)?to\s+\w/i`

**P2: `discuss about` / `mention about` (redundant preposition)**
- Pattern: "let's discuss about this issue" → "let's discuss this issue"
- Frequency: 3-5%; FP risk: very low (about after discuss/mention is always redundant)
- Regex: `/\b(discuss|mention|explain|consider|examine)\s+about\b/i`
