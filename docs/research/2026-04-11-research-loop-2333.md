### 3. Chronicle Canvas — deltaY Clamping (LOW complexity, LOW risk)

**Current state:** `applyZoomDelta` uses `ZOOM_SPEED = 0.002`, making the exponential formula self-normalizing:
- Mouse wheel `deltaY=100` → `exp(-0.2)` → 18% zoom change
- Trackpad `deltaY=3` → `exp(-0.006)` → 0.6% zoom change (many events per gesture = smooth)

**Research finding (Steve Ruiz / tldraw):** Clamping deltaY to ±10 is recommended when `ZOOM_SPEED` is ~0.01 or higher. With `ZOOM_SPEED = 0.002`, clamping to ±10 would make mouse wheel zoom only 2% per tick — far too slow.

**Verdict:** No change needed. The low `ZOOM_SPEED` value already normalizes the mouse/trackpad delta difference. The existing `ctrlKey` gate correctly separates pinch from two-finger scroll.
