**Problem**: Only catches "play important role". Misses these equally frequent CLEC-documented errors:
- "play key role" (without "a")
- "play crucial role" (without "a/an")
- "play vital role" (without "a")
- "play significant role" (without "a")
- "play major role" (without "a")
- "play central role" (without "a/an")

**Corrected L59A proposal** — expand the existing line 391 regex:

**FP analysis**:
- "plays an important role" → "an" breaks the `adjective role` adjacency ✓ (no match)
