## Loop 32 — Candidate Proposals

### P1: `one of the + singular noun` (missing plural)
- Error: "one of the reason" → "one of the reasons"
- Source: Chinese doesn't mark plural morphologically — 原因之一 → "one of the reason"
- Regex: `/\bone\s+of\s+the\s+([a-z]+)\b(?!s\b)/i` with countable noun list
- Frequency: ~10-12% Chinese L1 essays
- FP risk: LOW (~1%) — "one of the best" (superlative) needs guard

### P2: `as + adjective + as possible` dropped (missing second "as")
- Error: "as soon possible" / "as fast possible" → "as soon as possible"
- Source: Chinese 尽快 → learners omit second "as"  
- Regex: `/\bas\s+(soon|fast|quick|early|late|long|far|much|many|good|well|possible)\s+possible\b/i`
- Frequency: ~4-6%
- FP risk: ~0% — no native English omits the second "as"

### P3: `take someone long time` → `take someone a long time`
- Error: "It takes me long time" → "It takes me a long time"
- Source: 花很长时间 → missing article before "long time"
- Regex: `/\btake(?:s|n|ing)?\s+(?:me|you|him|her|them|us|it)\s+long\s+time\b/i`
- Frequency: ~4-5%
- FP risk: ~0%

### P4: `it is no doubt` → `there is no doubt` / `it is undeniable`
- Error: "It is no doubt that..." → "There is no doubt that..." or "Undoubtedly..."
- Source: Chinese 毫无疑问 → "it is no doubt" calque
- Regex: `/\bit\s+is\s+no\s+doubt\b/i`
- Frequency: ~3-5%
- FP risk: ~0%
