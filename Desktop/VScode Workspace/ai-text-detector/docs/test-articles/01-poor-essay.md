# Why School Should Change

School is boring and it needs to change because it is not good for students and everyone knows this already. I think school is bad because we sit in class all day and we dont learn anything useful and then we go home and do homework which is also boring and pointless.

Teachers just talk at you the whole time and you have to sit there and listen and you cant do anything about it. My friend Jake says the same thing he thinks school is a waste of time too. Every single day is the same thing you wake up go to school sit in a chair come home do homework go to sleep and then do it all over again. Its like being trapped.

Another thing is that school doesnt teach you real life stuff. Like nobody teaches you how to do taxes or buy a house or get a job. They just make you learn about old dead people and math equations you will never use. I dont understand why we have to learn about the civil war when I could be learning about how to manage money. It makes no sense.

Also the food in the cafeteria is terrible. And the school day is too long. And we dont get enough breaks. Everything about school is just bad. Some people say school is important but I disagree because I know plenty of people who didnt finish school and they are doing fine. My uncle dropped out and he has a good job now so that proves school isnt that important.

In conclusion school needs to change because it is boring and doesnt teach us anything useful. Someone should fix it because students are suffering. Thats all I have to say about it.
