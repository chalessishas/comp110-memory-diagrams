# The Day Everything Changed

There was a girl named Sarah and she was sad. She lived in a house with her mom and her dad. One day she woke up and she was still sad. She went to school and she was sad there too.

At school she had a friend named Mike. Mike was nice. He said hi to her and she said hi back. They went to class together. The teacher was talking about something but Sarah wasnt paying attention because she was sad. Mike noticed she was sad and asked her what was wrong. She said nothing was wrong but she was lying.

After school Sarah went home. Her mom was in the kitchen making dinner. Her mom asked how school was and Sarah said it was fine. Then she went to her room and sat on her bed. She was thinking about stuff. She was sad about a lot of things. Life was hard and she didnt know what to do about it.

The next day Sarah went to school again. This time something happened. A new kid came to school. The new kid was named Alex. Alex was interesting and different from everyone else. Alex talked to Sarah at lunch and Sarah felt a little bit better. They became friends.

After that Sarah wasnt as sad anymore. She had Alex and Mike and they all hung out together. Things got better for Sarah. She started to feel happy again. Her parents noticed she was happier and they were happy too. Everything was good now.

Sarah learned that sometimes you just need a friend to make things better. The end.
